## What should we consider as a score in our test?

- Elapsed time—Whole time to complete the test
- Connection time—Time to connect to the server
- Throughput—Number of requests per second
- Latency
- Query/Execution time—Time to execute a query
- Cache hit ratio—Percentage of queries that were served from the cache
- Disc I/O—Percentage of time spent reading and writing data to disk
- Write performance—Performance of writes
